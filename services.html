Web-Layout

This is the first step in creating a full website. It is deciding where all the aspects of a website should be placed by creating a hand drawn or computer generated wireframe that meets the needs and requirements of the client.

Web-Design

There are two parts in the design step. One is brand identity and the other is website design. Brand Identity is creating the client's own brand and logo that is unique from all others. Website Design is creating a web template that matches the logo of the client's company. These two parts are accomplished by designing the logo and the website in a program such as Photoshop or Fireworks.

Web-Programming

And finally programming is the most important part of designing a website. The design gets incorporated into the site by way of cascading stylesheets. Creating a stylesheet is how to get the site functioning exactly how the client wants it.
